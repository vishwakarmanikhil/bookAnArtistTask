About

I'm Rory, a freelance artist, muralist, graphic designer, CAD designer, engineer, welder, carpenter and problem solver. I live in London and have traveled the world for work. I am inspired by art, technology and the natural world; but also by fantasy and science fiction.


Committed to versatility, I choose not to be defined by a singular style. I often work to very specific client briefs but I am also able to create something unique if required.


I am happy to offer free consultation and estimates

Workshops, Interviews, Live painting events, Online Workshop, Canvas commissions, Custom designs for wallpapers or decals
